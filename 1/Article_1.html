Shift of Perspective ٩(＾◡＾)۶

The weirdest thing happened to me these last months, I was in a slump, a really deep one. A downward spiral filled with anxiousness and stress about basically everything.
Why?
I have no idea. I think it's just me, I make too much of things, like the future for instance. I don't like uncertainty, so I do everything in my power to ensure a 90% sucess rate. Except, failure can't be avoided, really. No matter how hard I try at..whatever, I'm bound to fail at some point But I didn't know that, until a few months ago. I've always attached myself worth to what I can give, naturally, I grew a tiny person inside my head telling me that I'm better than a lot of people because I do things better than them. Sure, I have my strong points, but this arrogance, which no one knew of, made me suffer.
I thought I deserved the elite spots, wherever I was, except I'm not special.
Appling to college abroad is teaching me something, that - yes - hard work is GOOD, shows that you're smart.. but it doesn't really mean anything.
Life was never about what we "produce", what we are, it's about experiencing : the good, the horrible and the average.
This helped me realize that sometimes my goals won't be reached, that I shouldn't EXPECT the world give to me just because I asked of it.
That's why I'm good now, with having drawbacks, messing up from time to time, taking days off. I'ts not that I don't care, It's that im not THAT inconvinienced by it anymore.
I still have that competitive bone in me, but another dog borrowing it for the day doesn't set me on edge anymore.
Well, that's all
